## Decision

Bind seven mechanical fields across the **15 Norse launch towers** (6 T1-T3 mainline + 6 T4 Demigod + 3 God) per the locked 7-field schema from R1 scope. Per-tower magnitudes within ±20% of the §4.11.5 tier-archetype DPS ladder; cd / range within tier-archetype bands; `attack_type` verbatim-confirms 2026-04-26 mapping; `status_proc` inherits the 2026-04-26 status-proc table with magnitude/duration authored per tier-intensity (T1-T3 baseline → T4 ~1.4× intensity → God paired alternation per locked 2-type Fusion model). All 15 rows carry `summon_anchor: ragnar` affinity-target tag — Norse = Summon lane lock per per-commander R1-R5 (Hard reversibility). `aux_slot_compat` defaults to §4.6a universal across all 15 rows; no civ-identity hook narrows the catalog at this round.

**Civ-identity hook (Norse = summon / called-warriors archetype):** Norse roster status-procs concentrate on **Bleed-density propagation** — Slashing × 2 sources at T1-T3 (Longhouse + Berserker Lodge) + Slashing × 2 sources at T4 (Björn Ironside + Lagertha) + Slashing carried into 2 of 3 Gods (Thor: Slashing+Crushing; Tyr: Slashing+Fire) = **6 of 15 Norse towers carry Slashing-Bleed**, the densest single-proc concentration across the three civs (vs Aztec 4 of 15 = Jaguar Warrior Hall + Jaguar Champion + Huitzilopochtli; Greek 3 of 15 = Theseus + Athena + Poseidon's-not-Slashing-correction-only-2-of-15 = Theseus + Athena). The summon archetype reads through the **cleave-propagation interaction** between Bleed and Ragnar's summoned warriors: Sons of Ragnar (passive — Norse-civ towers within 2-cell hex-distance of the anointed Norse tower spawn 1 Heathen Warrior every 8s, capped at 4 alive at once) frontline-cleave the Bleed-stacked enemies that enter melee, propagating Bleed across cleave-targets. Berserk (active, 30s CD) doubles summon-cap to 8 + +30% summon damage for 12s. Great Heathen Army (signature, once-per-match free) summons 8 Heathen Warriors @ 180 DPS × 12s lifespan, Slashing + frontal cleave + Bleed, 1.5× HP_std(R), auto-advance toward path frontline (~30,000 burst-equivalent + DOT). The Bleed-density-high choice in tower magnitudes feeds the cleave-summon interaction: more enemies entering the summon frontline already Bleeding means more cleave-Bleed-propagation per summon swing, compounding Bleed-DoT contribution beyond raw summon-DPS. Norse covers 5 of 7 attack types at T1-T3 (Poison and Divine absent at T1-T3; Divine arrives at T4 via Harald Bluetooth — the sole "warm-cold" Norse type bridge, mirroring how Theseus brought Slashing for Greek; Poison stays absent at launch — multi-civ play hook explicit). `cd` lands FAST overall (mainline 0.8–1.8s — center 1.15s, faster than Aztec mainline center 1.4s and Greek mainline center 1.7s); `range` lands SHORT-CENTER (mainline 2.5–4.5 cells with Berserker Lodge at 2.5 melee-shortest in any civ). `dps` clusters ±10% off baseline — kinetic-type towers at +10% (Longhouse, Longship Dock, Berserker Lodge); utility-type at-or-below baseline (Mead Hall stagger sole −10%; Rune Stone hex baseline; Forge baseline). This is the **falsifiable per-civ magnitude signature** R3's Loop 3 predicted: Norse cd-band-low + Bleed-density-high + dps-band-centered (no economy compounding pushes variance up; no lockdown burst-stagger pushes variance down).

**Per-commander affinity-target tag (`summon_anchor: ragnar`).** All 15 Norse rows tag the receiving side of Ragnar's three-ability kit:
- **Sons of Ragnar** (passive): every Norse-civ tower within 2-cell hex-distance of the anointed Norse tower contributes 1 Heathen Warrior every 8s spawn-tick (capped 4 alive). Tag binds the target-side of a Norse-civ-tower-aura interface — the spawn-eligibility per tower.
- **Berserk** (active, 30s CD): for 12s, summon-cap doubles to 8 + +30% summon damage. Tag binds the eligibility for the cap-extension.
- **Great Heathen Army** (signature, once-per-match free): instant 8 Heathen Warrior summon, 12s lifespan, 180 DPS each + Bleed + frontal cleave. Towers with `summon_anchor: ragnar` provide the spawn-anchor cells the warriors auto-advance from.

The tag does NOT cross civ lanes — no Greek or Aztec tower carries `summon_anchor: ragnar` (would be a cascade violation against the per-commander R1-R5 Hard-reversibility lane locks).

## Per-tier reading

**T1 paragraph.** The Norse T1 floor reads as a coverage roster across 5 of 7 attack types: Slashing × 2 (Longhouse + Berserker Lodge), Crushing (Mead Hall), Arcane (Rune Stone), Piercing (Longship Dock), Fire (Forge). Poison and Divine are permanently absent at T1-T3 (Divine arrives at T4 via Harald Bluetooth; Poison stays absent at launch — multi-civ play hook). T1 dps lands at 18–22 (band-low at 18 for Mead Hall stagger, band-high at 22 for kinetic-type Slashing/Piercing).

**T2 paragraph.** Merging two same-civ same-tier T1s produces a T2 at 60 dmg/sec baseline; Norse T2 cluster sits at 54–64 (median 64 — band-high at +10% for Longhouse, Longship Dock, Berserker Lodge; baseline 60 for Rune Stone and Forge; band-low 54 for Mead Hall). Layer Berserker Lodge-T2 at the same aura position (cd 0.8s, dps 64) and the Bleed-application count doubles. The T2 tier is where Hard 75% matchup-correctness threshold (per §4.11.8) starts mattering — Norse covers 5 of 7 attack types at T2, equivalent to Greek's 5 and one less than Aztec's 6, so matchup-correctness ceiling is comparable to Greek (slightly under Aztec at this tier).

## Reason chosen

The 15 Norse rows author the Summon lane lock per-commander R1-R5 Ragnar spec into the per-tower target-side. Three specific bindings hold:

1. **`summon_anchor: ragnar` is uniform across all 15 Norse rows** (mirroring R2 and R3's per-civ uniformity for `controlled_by: leonidas` and `economy_target: montezuma_ii`). The tag binds the Norse-civ-tower-aura interface eligibility for Sons of Ragnar (passive 2-cell spawn aura), Berserk (active 30s CD cap-extension), and Great Heathen Army (signature once-per-match 8-summon burst). At runtime, each Norse tower's spawn-anchor cell contributes to the summon spawn-eligibility per the tag.
2. **dps-band variance direction expresses summon archetype.** Kinetic-type towers (Slashing × 2 + Piercing × 1 at T1-T3) sit at +10% baseline — fast Slashing/Piercing cadence is the Bleed-density / armor-shred enabler that feeds cleave-summon throughput. Utility-type towers (Mead Hall stagger −10%, Rune Stone hex baseline, Forge baseline) sit at-or-below — the value comes from the proc kind (stagger pinning targets in cleave zone, hex slowing enemy attack-cadence to extend summon survival, burn-splash multi-target feeding cleave propagation), not the raw dps. T4 cluster centers near baseline 540 with kinetic T4s slightly above (Björn 600, Lagertha 600, Leif 580) and utility T4s at-or-near baseline (Sigurd 520, Beowulf 540, Harald 540).
