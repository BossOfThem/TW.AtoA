### Cross-civ parity reconciliation (R2 Greek's predicted Aztec aggregate, corrected at this round)

R2 Greek's cross-civ parity hook predicted Aztec's matchup_affinity as: STRONG vs Beast / STRONG vs Spirit / **NEUTRAL vs Shielded** / **WEAK vs Unarmored** / NEUTRAL-tilted-favorable vs Colossal. R3's actual aggregation against the locked 2026-04-26 RPS matrix and the per-tower R3 + RN type-coverage tables produces: STRONG vs Beast / STRONG vs Spirit / **WEAK vs Shielded** / **STRONG vs Unarmored** / NEUTRAL-tilted-favorable vs Colossal. The Beast/Spirit STRONG and Colossal NEUTRAL-tilted-favorable bins survive exactly. The Shielded and Unarmored bins **invert relative to R2's prediction**.

The reconciliation traces to the 2026-04-26 RPS row for Unarmored: only Crushing self-penalizes vs Unarmored (line 70 + 91 of the 2026-04-26 mapping); Piercing is neutral vs Unarmored, not unfavorable. R2's Aztec-vs-Unarmored prediction inferred Piercing as a self-penalty cell vs Unarmored, which the locked RPS does not specify. Aztec's structural Crushing-zero (the explicit multi_civ_play_hook from the per-tower RN audit) therefore resolves to **zero Unarmored liability** (no Crushing means no self-penalty cells vs Unarmored) and to **half-density Shielded coverage** (Aztec brings only Piercing of the Piercing+Crushing favored-pair, vs Greek's 7-of-15 paired-counter cell). The Crushing-hole's matchup-impact is at Shielded, not at Unarmored.

R2's Aztec parity hook stands corrected at the Shielded ↔ Unarmored bin pair; the **"Beast/Spirit specialist + Crushing-coverage hole + kill-multiplication identity"** pattern R2 forward-predicted is **substantively confirmed** — the Crushing-hole was correctly named, only its matchup-impact landing was misattributed (R2 attributed it to Unarmored as a Piercing-self-penalty inference; R3 correctly resolves it to Shielded as a missing-favored-counter resolution). No cascade-violation flag — R2 was a forward-prediction explicitly invited by R1 scope to be validated or flagged at R3, and R3 flags + reconciles the binning at exactly the layer where the falsification mechanism was designed to live. Greek-vs-Aztec cross-civ complementarity at the matchup-aggregate layer is **stronger** under the corrected binning than under R2's prediction: Aztec's Shielded-WEAK directly inverts Greek's Shielded-STRONG (densest paired-counter cell of any civ), making the cross-civ-play case for Greek+Aztec partner-civilization composition more legible, not less. The Unarmored bin is no longer a Greek-fills-Aztec gap (since Aztec is itself STRONG vs Unarmored at all tiers); the cross-civ play story shifts to "Aztec covers Beast/Unarmored swarm; Greek covers Shielded/Colossal walls; both share Spirit coverage; Norse covers the Crushing-rich Shielded backstop both Aztec lacks and Greek already brings."

### 2. `identity_hook`

Aztec is the **economy / yield-stack civilization** — its identity reads through the temporal compounding of three intersecting kill-multiplication procs against multi-target wave compositions held inside Montezuma II's Sun Offering 4-cell active zone. The per-tower magnitude signature centers (dps T2-T3 cluster +10% off the §4.11.5 ladder for 4 of 6 mainline towers — yield-stack-mid-tier-consolidation per per-tower R3 reading; cd median 1.4s — fastest-of-three-civs centered; range median 4.0 mainline — moderate, no extreme outliers; Sacrificial Altar 5-stack toxin sole stacking-proc per 2026-04-26 lock) express *kill-rate compounding over peak burst*: Poison toxin DoT ticks 2-3% max-HP/sec per stack across enemy-lifespan and saturates 5-cap on slow heavy targets in ~3s, Fire burn DoT runs 4%/sec for 3-4s with 25-30% adjacent splash multiplying enemy-hits per cast, Slashing bleed DoT at 5-7% weapon-damage/sec for 3s propagates across damaged-target-counts, Arcane hex −25%-to-−35% attack/cast-speed for 2-3s extends enemy time-in-aura compounding the Blood Tribute window, and Divine smite +10-12% true damage layered with the Pyramid/High Priest-King/Quetzalcoatl Divine source-density carries Spirit and PvE-stealth-reveal secondary duty. The status-proc archetype is **kill-multiplication** — three distinct compounding vectors (per-stack DoT saturation / per-cast splash multiplication / per-target propagation) intersect on the active wave inside Sun Offering 4-cell zone, where each kill triggers Blood Tribute's +15% bonus passive layer + Sun Offering's +35% active layer + (post-signature) Great Sacrifice's permanent +5% yield layer + (per §4.6a) Economy-Bonus aux's +25% layer for **base × 2.039× per-kill yield** in active overlap (per per-commander R5 audit math, post-Lever-2). The matchup_affinity profile (STRONG vs Beast + Spirit + Unarmored, WEAK vs Shielded) is *the same shape* as the yield-stack archetype's natural geometry: kill-multiplication procs return their highest leverage on multi-target swarm-and-myth wave compositions (Beast clusters / Spirit waves / Unarmored swarms — three of five armor classes) and their lowest leverage on single-priority elite-armor walls (Shielded mid-bosses where stacking-proc-saturation amortizes poorly against single-target HP-pools), and Aztec's per-tower magnitudes don't fight this geometry but lean into it via the dps-band-high mid-tier consolidation (T2-T3 4-of-6-towers at +10% baseline expresses Sun Offering's mid-tier amortization-budget per per-tower R3 spec) and the structural Crushing-zero (no Crushing means no Shielded coverage means no temptation to over-extend the yield-stack into matchups it can't compound on). Montezuma II's three abilities each target a different point in the yield-cycle: Blood Tribute's +15% bonus-Tribute-per-kill 2-cell civ-aura is the *passive baseline* (every Aztec-civ tower within hex-distance of the anointed Aztec tower applies the bonus to every kill), Sun Offering's 4s window + 4-cell radius +35% bonus-on-Aztec-tower-kills active is the *spike multiplier* (player times the cast for peak wave-density to compound the per-kill bonus across max kill-count in zone), The Great Sacrifice's once-per-match free signature is the *permanent floor lift* (post-Lever-2: 300 T lump immediately + permanent +5% Aztec-tower yield board-wide for rest of match — kill-multiplication becomes match-permanent yield-multiplication). Read together — civ-aggregate matchup leaning toward Beast/Spirit/Unarmored breadth-of-coverage, per-tower magnitudes centered on kill-rate compounding via stacking + splash + propagation, status-proc archetype dominated by per-kill multiplicative-yield triggers, commander synergy converting kill-rate into match-permanent income — Aztec is mechanically identifiable as the civ where *the more enemies that die, the more bonus Tribute compounds, and the more T4 promotes arrive earlier*, and where the player's optimal play is **routing swarm-and-myth waves through Aztec-tower clusters with Sun Offering timed to peak wave-density and Great Sacrifice spent at R10-R15 to compound the +5% permanent multiplier across the full late-match yield window**.

### 3. `signature_creep_types`

Aztec specializes against **Beast** and **Spirit** armor classes (archetype-grain only — per-creep-row specifics defer to the §4.7 enemy direction lock). The pairing reads cleanly: Beast is the +25% Poison-stacking + Fire-splash convergence (Sacrificial Altar / Blood Priest / Tezcatlipoca Poison; Temple of the Sun / Nanahuatzin / Huitzilopochtli Fire — the 6-of-15 paired-counter density, densest Beast-counter cell of any civ × armor at launch), while Spirit is the +25% Arcane hex sink (Feathered Serpent Temple / Serpent-Priest / Quetzalcoatl) + the Divine smite +10%-true secondary (Pyramid / High Priest-King + Quetzalcoatl/Tezcatlipoca partials). Both armor classes share the *multi-target / sustained-engagement / swarm-or-channel-resistant* shape that kill-multiplication procs convert into compound advantage; both naturally surface at swarm-density waves (mid-round Beast clusters + Spirit channeling waves per the deferred §4.7 archetype roster). Aztec-against-Beast is the strongest civ × Beast cell at launch (Poison-stacking sole stacking-proc layer + Fire-splash adjacent layer + zero Arcane self-penalty in the active stack since Arcane towers naturally route to Spirit targets when matchup-routing is correct). Aztec-against-Spirit is favorable through dual-axis coverage (Arcane + Divine smite — both the §4.10 RPS-favorable type and the locked Divine-smite proc layer hit Spirit cleanly). Aztec does *not* specialize against Unarmored as a *signature* role despite the favorable matchup — Unarmored is structurally favorable (zero Crushing liability) but isn't the kill-multiplication-archetype's *signature target* the way Beast/Spirit are (Unarmored swarm waves don't benefit from the stacking-DoT saturation as much as multi-HP Beast/Spirit waves do; the bonus is in raw kill-rate-throughput, not in compounding-DoT-per-target). Aztec does not specialize against Colossal (NEUTRAL-tilted; Greek owns the Colossal lane) or Shielded (signature gap, see field 4).

### 4. `multi_civ_play_hook`

Aztec's structural type-hole at the launch-roster grain is **Crushing** (zero sources across 15 towers — confirmed by 2026-04-26 attack-type mapping + per-tower R3 + per-tower RN audit type-coverage table). The Crushing-hole resolves to a Shielded-armor matchup gap (Shielded is +25% Piercing + +25% Crushing per 2026-04-26 RPS, so Aztec brings only one-of-two favored counters — paired-counter density is half what a balanced Shielded-coverage civ would have). The hole has no second-order Unarmored impact (Crushing self-penalizes vs Unarmored; Aztec lacking Crushing means Aztec lacks the Unarmored liability that Greek/Norse carry — see Cross-civ parity reconciliation above for R2-prediction-correction). Cross-civ play fills the hole: **Greek Crushing-density** (Acropolis T1-T3 + Hercules T4 + Zeus God Crushing-partial — 2 of 15 dedicated + 1 partial = 3 of 15 effective Crushing sources, one of two civs with non-zero Crushing) and **Norse Crushing-density** (Mead Hall T1-T3 + Beowulf T4 + Thor God Crushing-partial — 2 of 15 dedicated + 1 partial = 3 of 15 effective, the densest Crushing roster of any civ when partial sources are weighted) directly answer the Shielded-WEAK matchup. Both cross-civ partner-civs bring Crushing, but they bring it through different per-commander interfaces (Greek's Acropolis/Hercules carries `controlled_by: leonidas` lockdown-stack value; Norse's Mead Hall/Beowulf carries `summon_anchor: ragnar` summon-cleave-anchor value) — the multi-civ-play decision is which partner-civ's secondary-effects best complement the Aztec player's chosen Sun Offering / Great Sacrifice match-arc. The deferred Foresight-coin civ-pair model (Phase 4 §4.x, OPEN) and the Follow-up #6 patch-1 commander expansion (per-civ patch commanders bringing additional type-coverage) are the structural release valves for the Crushing-hole — Aztec-only single-civ play remains intentionally walled by Shielded-heavy R15 Elite waves and R20-R25 Elite-Heavy compositions, and the design intent (per the per-tower RN audit's "type-holes are not bugs" verdict and per the 2026-04-25 ratification's multi-civ-future commitment) is that *Aztec's Crushing-hole is the gameplay reason to engage cross-civ play with Greek or Norse, not a balance debt to patch*. The **cultural-load surface** of this multi-civ-play hook merits explicit acknowledgment: Aztec's matchup-WEAK at Shielded combined with Aztec's matchup-STRONG-tilted-favorable across Beast/Spirit/Unarmored makes Aztec the *naturally-collaborative* civ at the matchup layer (Aztec wants partner-civs more than Greek or Norse do, because its WEAK cell is on the structural-elite-wall lane that other civs always bring counters to), which the per-commander R5 Montezuma II Economy lane lock matches (Blood Tribute's per-kill bonus has highest leverage when MORE Aztec-civ towers are present in the player roster — Aztec is the civ that *wants to own large fractions of the roster within a partner-civ build*). This is purely a mechanical-content reading of the matchup + commander structure; **no cultural-flavor surface direction is implied or generated** (Follow-up #5 hard-gate carries forward unchanged; Aztec heaviest cultural-load center vigilance held).

## 3x debug loop on identity_hook construction

**Loop 1 — aggressive critique.** The identity_hook paragraph is a long synthesis sentence that *names* every input field (matchup_affinity Beast/Spirit/Unarmored breadth + Shielded gap; per-tower cd median 1.4s + dps-band-high T2-T3; status-proc archetype as "kill-multiplication"; Montezuma II three-stage yield-cycle) but the "kill-multiplication-weighted lifespan-compounding" framing might collapse into the *same* "lifespan-compounding" claim that R2's Greek identity_hook leveraged for "deceleration-weighted lifespan-compounding." Both civs claim that *the longer enemies live, the more procs they eat*, and then claim the *kind* of compounding distinguishes them (deceleration vs kill-multiplication). But Greek's deceleration-weighted IS itself a kill-multiplier (slower enemies eat more shots → more kills inside the same tower window) and Aztec's kill-multiplication-weighted IS itself partially-deceleration (Arcane hex slows attack/cast-speed; Slashing-bleed slows movement-via-DoT-over-time-in-zone). The two civs' proc-shape framings might be re-descriptions of the same underlying mechanic (sustained tower-damage-on-target-over-time) rather than empirically distinct shapes. Worse: the R2-promised falsification gate ("if R3/R4 identity_hooks land on the same deceleration-weighted shape as Greek, the schema's identity_hook field is hollow and RN audit flags it") might be too lenient — distinguishing "deceleration-weighted" from "kill-multiplication-weighted" might be a verbal distinction that doesn't survive a magnitude-layer audit. The identity_hook risks being prose-decoration distinguished only by the surface-flavor of its locked content-skeleton names (Sacrificial Altar / Sun Offering / Great Sacrifice evoke "sacrifice/yield/economy"; Phalanx / Last Stand / Spartan-Training evoke "lockdown/stand/control") — Follow-up #5 ambient-cultural-flavor doing the work the mechanical-content prose claims to do.

**Loop 2 — steelman.** The deceleration ↔ kill-multiplication distinction holds at the magnitude layer, not the verbal layer. Greek's deceleration-weighted means: per-target-time-in-zone increases via slow/stun/hex-cast-slow procs, and per-target-damage-per-second remains roughly constant — so total per-target damage scales linearly with time-in-zone. Aztec's kill-multiplication-weighted means: per-cast-target-count increases via splash + propagation + 5-stack saturation procs, and per-target-DoT-tick-rate scales with stack-count not with target-time-in-zone alone — so total per-cast yield scales as (target-count × stack-saturation × per-kill-bonus-multiplier) which is a *different mathematical shape* from Greek's per-target-time integral. Empirically: Greek's Last-Stand 12s zone-denial × Zeus 5-cell-range overlap = ~13,200 raw damage in zone over 12s (per per-tower R2 spec) — this is per-target-time-integral. Aztec's Sun Offering 4-cell × Huitzilopochtli inside aura post-Sacrifice = base × 2.039× per-kill yield (per per-commander R5 audit math) — this is per-cast-multiplier-on-per-kill-yield. The two equations are not re-descriptions of each other — they have different free variables (Greek scales with target-time-in-zone; Aztec scales with target-count × per-kill-bonus-multiplier). The verbal proc-shape distinction maps to a magnitude-layer distinction the per-commander R5 + per-tower R2-R3 specs already bound. The "Follow-up #5 ambient-cultural-flavor doing the work" critique is the genuine surface-direction risk — but the paragraph as written stays at mechanical-content language ("kill-multiplication," "compounding-DoT," "per-stack DoT saturation," "splash multiplies kills-per-cast," "match-permanent yield-multiplication") without invoking pose / VO / ritual surface direction; the ambient tone evoked by the locked content-skeleton names (Sacrificial Altar, Blood Priest, Sun Offering, Great Sacrifice) is the locked-name ambient doing what the 2026-04-25 ratification + per-commander R5 one-pagers + per-tower R3 explicit acknowledgment of "Aztec heaviest cultural-load center" already permitted; no NEW surface direction prose is generated this round.
