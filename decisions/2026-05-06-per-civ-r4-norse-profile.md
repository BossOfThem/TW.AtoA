### Cross-civ parity reconciliation (R3 Aztec's predicted Norse aggregate, validated at this round)

R3 Aztec's cross-civ parity hook predicted Norse's matchup_affinity as: STRONG vs Shielded / STRONG vs Unarmored / **NEUTRAL vs Beast** / WEAK vs Spirit / NEUTRAL vs Colossal. R4's actual aggregation against the locked 2026-04-26 RPS matrix and the per-tower R4 + RN type-coverage tables produces: STRONG vs Shielded / STRONG vs Unarmored / **NEUTRAL-tilted-favorable vs Beast** / WEAK vs Spirit / NEUTRAL-tilted-favorable vs Colossal. The Shielded STRONG, Unarmored STRONG, and Spirit WEAK bins survive **exactly as predicted**. The Beast and Colossal bins **shift from R3's flat NEUTRAL to NEUTRAL-tilted-favorable**.

The reconciliation traces to the God-tier Fusion contributions R3's flat-NEUTRAL prediction did not weight: at Beast, Tyr's God-tier Slashing+Fire alternation (~30% splash + 4s burn duration + bleed propagation) layered with Sigurd T4 expanded-splash + 4s burn pushes the Fire-vs-Beast effective source-count to 3 of 15 (vs R3's implicit 1-2 weighting that overlooked God-tier partials), tilting the bin upward from flat NEUTRAL. At Colossal, Odin God-tier Divine-Piercing alternation provides an anti-boss-armor profile (Divine smite +10% true × Piercing armor-shred −2/3s) that pushes Colossal upward despite the pre-T4 Divine absence; Crushing-stagger pinning + Slashing-Bleed sustained-DOT through summon-cleave-pin layer (Thor God-tier stagger-pin × bleed alternation) compound positively on R30 boss-class. Both bin shifts are **upward tilts**, not category re-categorizations, and operate within R3's anticipated direction of effect — R3's "Norse middle-of-three-civs at Colossal, less effective at Beast than Aztec but functional through high-cd-band-low kill-rate" reading **survives intact** under the corrected weighting.

R3's Norse parity hook stands **substantively confirmed** at all five bins; the **"Shielded/Unarmored specialist + Spirit-and-Divine-pre-T4 hole + summon-cleave-propagation identity"** pattern R3 forward-predicted is **fully validated** — the Shielded/Unarmored STRONG signature, the Spirit WEAK gap, and the orthogonal complement to Greek (Colossal/Shielded + Beast hole) and Aztec (Beast/Spirit/Unarmored + Shielded hole) all hold under direct aggregation. The Beast and Colossal NEUTRAL → NEUTRAL-tilted-favorable shifts are within-band refinements, not direction reversals. No cascade-violation flag — R3 was a forward-prediction explicitly invited by R1 scope to be validated or flagged at R4, and R4 validates the structural pattern while flagging the within-band God-tier-partial weighting that R3's prediction implicitly underweighted. Three-civ cross-complementarity at the matchup-aggregate layer holds exactly as R3's "Greek covers Crushing-rich Shielded backstop both Aztec lacks and Greek already brings" framing predicted: Aztec covers Beast/Unarmored swarm; Greek covers Shielded/Colossal walls; Norse covers Shielded/Unarmored elite-and-swarm; **all three civs share Shielded coverage through different proc-mechanics** (Greek paired Piercing+Crushing temporal-compounding lockdown; Norse paired Piercing+Crushing summon-cleave-Bleed-propagation; Aztec single-Piercing-only — the WEAK cell that defines Aztec's structural complement to Greek+Norse partner-civ composition).

### 2. `identity_hook`

Norse is the **summon / called-warriors civilization** — its identity reads through the spatial multiplication of additional bodies on the field via Ragnar's three-stage summon cycle, where Bleed-density propagation across the Heathen-Warrior cleave frontline compounds tower-DPS into summon-DPS-coverage that scales by warrior-count × cleave-radius × per-target-Bleed-stack-density. The per-tower magnitude signature centers (dps median ±10% off the §4.11.5 ladder for kinetic-vs-utility split — kinetic +10%, utility −10% to baseline; cd median 1.15s — fastest-of-three-civs centered, Berserker Lodge + Longship Dock tied at 0.8s; range median 3.5 mainline — shortest-of-three-civs centered, Berserker Lodge 2.5 melee-shortest in any civ; Slashing source-density 6 of 15 — densest single-proc concentration of any civ at launch) express *force-multiplication through additional-bodies-on-field over peak burst or compounding-yield*: Slashing-bleed DoT runs 5%/sec/3s at T1-T3 → 7%/sec/3s at T4 (intensity bump per 2026-04-26 Follow-up #4) and propagates across cleave-target groups via Sons-of-Ragnar Heathen-Warrior frontal-cleave swings, Crushing-stagger −20%-to-−30% move-speed/1-1.5s pins targets inside the summon-cleave engagement window for full Bleed-tick coverage, Piercing armor-shred −1/3s-to-−2/3s refreshes enable full-damage Bleed-stacking on R15 Elite Shielded armor that summons would otherwise bounce off, Arcane hex −25% attack/cast-speed/2s extends enemy time-in-cleave-zone compounding per-cleave Bleed-application count, Fire burn DoT runs 4%/sec for 3s at T1-T3 → 6%/sec for 4s at T4 with 25%-30% adjacent splash multiplying enemy-hits per cast inside the cleave group, and Divine smite +12% true damage layered with Harald Bluetooth's T4 Divine source-count + Odin's God-tier Divine-Piercing alternation carries Spirit and Colossal-boss secondary duty (the sole T4-onset civ for its Divine bucket). The status-proc archetype is **force-multiplication via cleave-Bleed-propagation** — the temporal compounding shape (Bleed lifespan-stack on the per-target axis × cleave propagation on the per-swing axis × summon-count escalation on the per-stage-of-summon-cycle axis) intersects on the active wave inside Sons-of-Ragnar 2-cell aura, where each additional summoned warrior multiplies cleave swings per second × each cleave swing propagates Bleed across 2-3 adjacent enemies × each propagated Bleed compounds DoT-tick contribution beyond raw summon-DPS (per per-tower R4 T3 paragraph: ~720 dps from 3-4 T3 tower cluster + 4-8 Heathen Warriors @ 180 DPS each on cleave frontline = ~1,440-2,160 effective dps with Bleed propagation — peak summon-archetype force-multiplier). The matchup_affinity profile (STRONG vs Shielded + Unarmored, WEAK vs Spirit) is *the same shape* as the summon archetype's natural geometry: cleave-Bleed-propagation procs return their highest leverage on multi-target wave compositions where summon-cleave-radius covers multiple adjacent targets simultaneously (Unarmored swarms where 6-of-15 Slashing-density at +25% RPS converts directly into peak kill-rate × cleave-propagation; Shielded elite-walls where Crushing-stagger pins the elite into the cleave-Bleed-saturation window for the Piercing-armor-shred refresh cycle to enable full-damage Bleed compounding) and their lowest leverage on dispersed-priority single-target Spirit channeling waves where summon-cleave-radius hits zero adjacent targets (and where Norse's pre-T4 Arcane-1 + Divine-0 source-count walls direct-RPS coverage anyway), and Norse's per-tower magnitudes don't fight this geometry but lean into it via the cd-band-fastest-in-any-civ (mainline mid 1.15s expresses fast Bleed-application cadence feeding cleave throughput) and the range-band-shortest-in-any-civ (mainline mid 3.5 cells; Berserker Lodge 2.5 melee-shortest expresses summon-archetype's preference for close engagement to anchor warrior frontline) and the structural Spirit-and-Divine-pre-T4 hole (no early Divine means no temptation to over-extend the summon-cleave into matchups it can't cleave-propagate on). Ragnar Lothbrok's three abilities each target a different point in the summon-cycle: Sons of Ragnar's passive 1-Heathen-Warrior-every-8s spawn at 4-cap from any Norse-civ tower within 2-cell hex-distance is the *passive baseline* (every Norse-civ tower within hex-distance of the anointed Norse tower contributes a spawn-anchor cell, building a 4-warrior cleave frontline over the first ~32s of engagement), Berserk's 4s active 8-cap-extension + +30% summon damage at 30s CD is the *spike multiplier* (player times the cast for peak wave-density to compound the cleave-Bleed across max-cleave-target-count in zone with bonus per-summon damage), The Great Heathen Army's once-per-match free signature 8-Heathen-Warrior 12s instant burst (180 DPS each + Slashing + frontal cleave + Bleed + 1.5× HP_std(R) auto-advance toward path frontline) is the *spatial-overwhelm window* (instant 8-warrior force-multiplier dropped at the wave-frontline + Berserk overlap permits temporary 16-warrior peak-cleave state for 12s — the canonical R25-R30 boss-killing window per per-tower R4 T4 paragraph). Read together — civ-aggregate matchup leaning toward Shielded/Unarmored elite-and-swarm coverage, per-tower magnitudes centered on cd-band-fastest cadence × Bleed-density-densest concentration × range-band-shortest engagement, status-proc archetype dominated by per-cleave-swing Bleed-propagation × per-summon-count force-multiplication, commander synergy converting tower-cadence into summoned-warrior-DPS-coverage that propagates across cleave-target groups — Norse is mechanically identifiable as the civ where *the more bodies in the summon frontline, the wider the cleave-Bleed propagation, and the more enemies share Bleed-stacks that compound DoT-tick contribution beyond raw summon-DPS*, and where the player's optimal play is **routing Shielded mid-bosses + Unarmored swarms through Norse-tower clusters with Sons-of-Ragnar 2-cell auras compounded across cluster cells, Berserk timed to peak wave-density inside cleave-zone, and Great Heathen Army spent at R25-R30 to overlap with Berserk for the 16-summon-cap window and exploit Tyr's God-tier bleed-burn-splash across the cleave group**.

### 4. `multi_civ_play_hook`

Norse's structural type-holes at the launch-roster grain are **Poison** (zero sources across 15 towers — confirmed by 2026-04-26 attack-type mapping + per-tower R4 + per-tower RN audit type-coverage table; shared with Greek's structural Poison-zero) and **Divine pre-T4** (zero T1-T3 mainline Divine source — Harald Bluetooth T4 is the earliest Norse Divine, mirroring Theseus T4 as Greek's earliest Slashing).
