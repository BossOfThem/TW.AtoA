## Decision

Bind seven mechanical fields across the **15 Aztec launch towers** (6 T1-T3 mainline + 6 T4 Demigod + 3 God) per the locked 7-field schema from R1 scope. Per-tower magnitudes within ±20% of the §4.11.5 tier-archetype DPS ladder; cd / range within tier-archetype bands; `attack_type` verbatim-confirms 2026-04-26 mapping; `status_proc` inherits the 2026-04-26 status-proc table with magnitude/duration authored per tier-intensity (T1-T3 baseline → T4 ~1.4× intensity → God paired alternation per locked 2-type Fusion model). **Civ-identity hook (Aztec = yield-stack / economy archetype):** Aztec roster status-procs concentrate on **kill-multiplication** — Poison stacking (the sole stacking proc per 2026-04-26 lock; ×2 sources in Aztec: Sacrificial Altar + Blood Priest), Fire splash adjacent (Temple of the Sun + Nanahuatzin — splash multiplies kill-count per cast), Slashing bleed propagation (Jaguar Warrior Hall + Jaguar Champion), and Divine smite peak vs Colossal R30 boss-class (Pyramid + High Priest-King). Aztec covers 6 of 7 attack types at T1-T3 (only Crushing absent; Crushing never arrives in Aztec — multi-civ play hook explicit). The yield-stack archetype reads through the **kill-rate compounding** of these procs inside Montezuma II's auras: Poison stacks tick small-damage many-times → many small kills → many `bonus_tribute_yield_in_active_zone` triggers + Blood Tribute base +15% bonus + permanent_tribute_yield_multiplier post-Sacrifice (+5%) + §4.6a Economy-Bonus aux (1 Div = +25%) compounds multiplicatively to base × 2.039× per kill in active overlap (per per-commander R5 audit, post-Lever-2). This is the economy signature: "many small ticks, many small kills, many small bonuses, layered, compounding." `cd` lands moderate-to-fast (mainline 0.9–2.0s; Aztec wants high kill-rate, not slow burst); `range` lands moderate (3–5 cells with Feathered Serpent Temple at 5 / Jaguar Warrior Hall at 3 close-quarters). `dps` clusters at the **upper edge** of the ±20% band at T2-T3 (mid-tier consolidation maximizes Sun Offering 4-cell active-zone amortization per per-commander R3 spec) — this is the **falsifiable per-civ magnitude signature** R2's Loop 3 predicted: Aztec dps-band-high at T2-T3 distinct from Greek's centered band.

**Per-commander affinity-target tag (`economy_target: montezuma_ii`).** All 15 Aztec rows tag the receiving side of Montezuma II's three-ability kit:
- **Blood Tribute** (passive): every Aztec-civ tower within 2-cell hex-distance of the anointed Aztec tower applies +15% bonus Tribute per kill, multiplicative w/ §4.6a Economy Bonus aux. Tag binds the target-side of an Aztec-tower-aura interface.
- **Sun Offering** (active, 30s CD): cast at player-aimed cell, 4s window, 4-cell radius, +35% bonus Tribute on Aztec-tower kills in zone during window. The `bonus_tribute_yield_in_active_zone` flag (per per-commander R5 close) reads from each Aztec tower at runtime; this round binds the receiving-side eligibility.
- **The Great Sacrifice** (signature, once-per-match free, post-Lever-2): instant 300 T lump + permanent +5% Aztec-tower yield board-wide for rest of match. The `permanent_tribute_yield_multiplier` flag (per per-commander R5 close) reads from each Aztec tower at runtime; this round binds the receiving-side eligibility.

The tag does NOT cross civ lanes — no Greek or Norse tower carries `economy_target: montezuma_ii` (would be a cascade violation against the per-commander R1-R5 Hard-reversibility lane locks).

## Per-tier reading

**T1 paragraph.** The Aztec T1 floor reads as a coverage roster across 6 of 7 attack types: Divine (Pyramid), Fire (Temple of the Sun), Slashing (Jaguar Warrior Hall), Piercing (Eagle Warrior Hall), Poison (Sacrificial Altar), Arcane (Feathered Serpent Temple). Crushing is permanently absent — multi-civ play hook. A new player picking Aztec lays Jaguar Warrior Hall + Eagle Warrior Hall for sustained kinetic kill-rate, drops a Sacrificial Altar early to start Poison-stacking on Beast / boss-class waves, and decides Pyramid / Temple of the Sun / Feathered Serpent Temple based on the wave-mix telegraph. cd-spread 0.9s (Jaguar Warrior Hall) → 2.0s (Temple of the Sun) means coverage-roster cadence is faster on average than Greek's (Aztec mainline mid cd 1.4s vs Greek 1.7s) — kill-rate compounding starts immediately at T1. T1 dps lands at 20–22 (band-low end clamped at baseline; band-high at +10% for 4 of 6 lines).

**T2 paragraph.** Merging two same-civ same-tier T1s produces a T2 at 60 dmg/sec baseline; Aztec T2 cluster sits at 60–66 (median 66 — band-high at +10%). The T2 tier is where the yield-stack archetype starts paying compounding dividends: a Jaguar Warrior Hall-T2 (cd 0.9s, dps 66) inside Blood Tribute 2-cell aura at peak wave density (~15 std runners per wave per §4.11.4) generates ~15 kills × +15% bonus Tribute per kill = ~2.25 bonus Tribute per Jaguar-T2-aided wave from passive alone; layer Sun Offering's 4-cell +35% active window during peak-density wave-quarter and the per-cast bonus surfaces. Per the per-commander R5 audit Hardcore breakpoint check, Aztec scaling is *more* valuable under Hardcore (income k-invariant per §4.11.2; HP scales with k → towers cost more value in DPS-equivalent → economy compounding amplifies). T2 is where Hard 75% matchup-correctness threshold (per §4.11.8) starts mattering — Aztec's diverse 6-type coverage (vs Greek's 5-type) gives 1 extra coverage axis, which translates to higher matchup-correctness ceiling.

**T3 paragraph.** Two T2 same-civ merges yield a T3 at 180 dmg/sec baseline; Aztec T3 cluster sits at 180–198 (median 198 — band-high at +10% for 4 of 6 lines). T3 is where the Aztec lockdown-AGAINST-economy-AGAINST-summon civ-archetype distinction surfaces sharpest: Greek T3 cluster of 3-4 towers inside Last-Stand zone produces ~720 dps lockdown contribution; Aztec T3 cluster of 3-4 towers inside Sun Offering active zone produces ~720+ dps damage **plus** ~+50% Tribute compounding (Blood Tribute +15% × Sun Offering +35% multiplicative on every kill in active overlap). At Hard expert R20-R25 cumulative-economy this translates to ~30-50% extra Tribute generation, which funds the T4 promote schedule one round earlier on average. T3 is where the Aztec player's competitive identity — "the player who has more T4s sooner" — emerges. cd-band 0.9–2.0s persists; Sacrificial Altar's 5-stack Poison-T3 against R15 Elite (1,839 HP × ε=0.6 in-range = ~1,100 HP eaten in-zone) saturates 5-stack toxin in ~3s, then ticks ~110 HP/sec damage per stack × 5 = 550 HP/sec — i.e., Poison-T3-stacked + base T3 dps 180 = ~730 effective dps against R15 Elites. Poison's value at T3 is the multiplier on slow heavy targets, not the raw dps.

**T4 Demigod paragraph.** T4 Demigods land at 540 dmg/sec baseline; Aztec T4 cluster sits at 480–600. Aztec T4 fills NO civ-roster type-holes (all 6 T1-T3 attack types persist into T4), but tier-up amplifies the yield-stack identity: Nanahuatzin's expanded Fire splash (30% adjacent vs T1-T3's 25%) widens the multi-kill envelope per cast; Jaguar Champion's 0.8s cd (faster than any Aztec T1-T3) maxes out kill-rate inside aura; Blood Priest's 5s-duration 5-stack toxin saturates faster on slower R30-class bosses. cd-band shifts down (0.8–1.8s vs T1-T3's 0.9–2.0s) — yield-stack archetype trends faster at T4 because per-kill economy bonuses dominate over per-shot peak burst. status_proc T4 intensity bumps (−2 armor / 7% bleed / 30% splash / 12% smite / 35% hex / 5s 3%/stack toxin) compound the per-kill bonus stack: a 5-stack-saturated Blood Priest at +15% Blood Tribute + +35% Sun Offering + +5% post-Sacrifice + +25% Economy aux on kill = base × 2.039× per-kill yield (per per-commander R5 audit math). Three of the six T4s (Nanahuatzin, Jaguar Champion, Eagle Champion, High Priest-King, Blood Priest, Serpent-Priest — all six) gate the three Aztec Fusions (3 distinct pairs).

**God paragraph.** Three Aztec Gods land at 1080 dmg/sec baseline; Aztec God cluster sits at 1080–1200 (median 1100 — slightly above Greek's 1050–1100 cluster, expressing yield-stack peak amplification at God-tier). Each God carries a 2-type alternating-per-shot proc set per locked 2026-04-26 Fusion model: Quetzalcoatl hex × smite, Huitzilopochtli burn-splash × bleed, Tezcatlipoca toxin × armor-shred. Range-band 4–5 cells (under Greek's 4.5–5.5 because Aztec yield-stack wants high kill-rate via cluster-coverage, not extreme range). cd-band 1.2–1.8s (under Greek's 1.2–2.2s — faster center). ## Reason chosen

The 15 Aztec rows author the Economy lane lock per-commander R1-R5 Montezuma II spec into the per-tower target-side. Three specific bindings hold:

1. **`economy_target: montezuma_ii` is uniform across all 15 Aztec rows** (mirroring R2's per-civ uniformity for `controlled_by: leonidas`). The tag binds the Aztec-civ-tower-aura interface eligibility for Blood Tribute (passive 2-cell aura), Sun Offering (active 4-cell zone), and Great Sacrifice (board-wide permanent multiplier post-Lever-2: 300 T lump + permanent +5% yield). At runtime, each Aztec tower's `bonus_tribute_yield_in_active_zone` flag (during Sun Offering) and `permanent_tribute_yield_multiplier` flag (post-Sacrifice) read from this tag.
2. **dps-band variance direction expresses yield-stack archetype.** 4 of 6 T1-T3 towers cluster at +10% baseline (Pyramid, Temple of the Sun, Jaguar Warrior Hall, Eagle Warrior Hall); 2 of 6 sit at baseline (Sacrificial Altar — Poison's stacking is the value, not raw dps; Feathered Serpent Temple — Arcane's hex/range utility is the value). T4 cluster shows the same pattern: rapid kinetic-type T4s above baseline (Nanahuatzin 600, Jaguar Champion 600, Eagle Champion 580); utility T4s at-or-below (Blood Priest 480, Serpent-Priest 520, High Priest-King 540 baseline). God cluster shifts upward overall (1080–1200 vs Greek's 1050–1100) — peak-economy amplification at God-tier expresses the Aztec yield-stack signature in its strongest form.
3. **cd / range bands match yield-stack archetype.** cd-band centers fast (mainline mid 1.4s; T4 mid 1.4s; God mid 1.5s — all faster than Greek's matching tiers). Range-band centers moderate (3–5 cells, no extreme-range outlier like Poseidon). The yield-stack archetype reads through **rapid kill-rate compounding inside moderate-range auras**, distinct from Greek's **moderate kill-rate compounding inside moderate-to-long-range zone-denial signatures**.
