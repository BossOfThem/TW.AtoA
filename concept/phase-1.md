# Phase 1 — Discovery

*What are we building, for whom, and why.*

Parent hub: [CONCEPT.md](../CONCEPT.md). Prior phase: — (root). Next: [phase-2.md](phase-2.md).

## 1.1 Vision statement

A tower wars game where every match plays as a compressed **mortal-to-mythic arc** grounded in **real-world cultures and their native myths**. Players command a legendary historical leader — **Leonidas of Sparta**, **Montezuma II of the Aztecs**, or **Ragnar Lothbrok of the Norse** — fielding a roster of culturally-recognizable mundane buildings (phalanxes, pyramids, longhouses) that reveal their mythic interior as the match progresses, culminating in a **Fusion endgame** where mortal heroes merge into named Gods of the civilization's pantheon (Zeus / Athena / Poseidon; Quetzalcoatl / Huitzilopochtli / Tezcatlipoca; Odin / Thor / Tyr). The tower you place in minute 1 still matters in minute 20 — it has merged and promoted through a tier ladder (T1 swarm → T2 mainline → T3 elite → T4 Demigod/Hero), and two of your T4 heroes may fuse into one of three locked Gods per civilization. The Commander you chose at login is a living identity across every match you play, with their own passive + short-CD active + long-CD active kit and their own signature flavor.

## 1.2 Target player

A strategy-game player who has finished *Kingdom Rush*, *Bloons TD 6*, and/or *Legion TD 2*. Comfortable with build-optimization loops and counter-picking. Interested in competitive multiplayer but also enjoys solo and co-op play. Values tight session length (5–25 minutes per match). Looking for a modern, polished take on the Warcraft 3 tower wars custom-map tradition with real single-player depth.

## 1.3 Core promise to the player

Three things, in order of importance:

1. **Commander identity.** The Commander you choose is yours. Across every match, your Commander's story, unlocks, and progression are visible and meaningful. You are not a generic avatar — you are a specific *historical* character (Leonidas, Montezuma II, Ragnar Lothbrok at launch; one per civ added in patch 1) with a specific 3-ability kit (passive + short-CD active + long-CD active) and a specific cultural flavor.
2. **Civilization persistence in-match.** The civilization you play in minute 1 still feels like the same civilization in minute 20 — even though every tower has promoted through the tier ladder (T1 swarm → T2 mainline → T3 elite → T4 Demigod/Hero) and two of your T4 heroes may have fused into a God. A Greek match looks Greek from first wave to final fusion; the same is true of Aztec and Norse.
3. **Multiple ways to play.** Solo vs AI, co-op horde, competitive lane wars, and commander-driven campaign — all built on one shared engine. No mode feels like an afterthought.

## 1.4 Success criteria for the vision

- Can be explained in one sentence to a Tower Wars fan and they nod.
- Solo mode is complete and enjoyable on its own, without any multiplayer.
- Has at least three unique mechanics no existing Tower Wars game ships with.
- Retains a player for ≥50 matches before content feels exhausted.
- Commander choice feels meaningful both mechanically and narratively.

## 1.5 Exit condition for Phase 1

Vision and promise are specific enough that any team member could restate them without looking at this doc. No further discovery work is needed to move on.
