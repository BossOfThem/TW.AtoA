## Scope

Cross-lineage hybrid units that unlock via board adjacency. Covers:

- Adjacency rules (what counts as "adjacent," duration threshold).
- Hybrid tech tree — how hybrids appear in the build menu once unlocked.
- Hybrid table — 20–30 entries spanning all age × lineage-pair combinations.
- Adjacent-lineage (common) vs opposite-lineage (rare, high-payoff) hybrids.
- The discovery mechanic — **OPEN BLOCKER from [CONCEPT §4.3](../CONCEPT.md)**. Three candidates: accept-rote, randomness, per-commander/per-account gating.

## To be written (Phase C pass)

Hinges on the discovery-mechanic resolution. Until that decision lands, this stage remains a stub.

## Open questions (seed list)

- **OPEN BLOCKER:** hybrid discovery mechanic (accept / randomize / gate).
- Hybrid table completeness at launch — all 20–30 or a starter 10 with rest earned/unlocked.
- Hybrid visual identity — does a hybrid inherit from both parents' art, or get its own?
- Do hybrids evolve through ages like pure-lineage towers, or are they terminal?
- Can a hybrid be the *input* to another hybrid (chain fusion)?

## Verification

- Players in playtesting report "discovering" a hybrid (not "looking up" one) on first encounter in ≥ 50% of cases.
- Hybrids meaningfully shift build strategy, not just add flavor.
