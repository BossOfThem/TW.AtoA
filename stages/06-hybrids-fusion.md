Hybridization is now a two-layer concept:

- **Layer 1 — within-civ tower merge** along the tier ladder (T1+T1 → T2, T2+T2 → T3, T3 → T4 Demigod/Hero). 18 T1-T3 towers + 18 T4 Demigods across 3 civilizations (6 per civ). Not "hybrids" in the cross-family sense — these are promotions.
- **Layer 2 — cross-T4-Demigod Fusion into named Gods.** **9 Gods, 3 per civ, via 9 locked 2-Demigod recipes.** Source T4 Demigods are **consumed** into one board slot. Fused God inherits both source attack types — Gods are the **only 2-type damage sources** in the game per [`decisions/2026-04-26-attack-type-mapping.md`](../decisions/2026-04-26-attack-type-mapping.md).
- **Cross-civ hybridization parked** as post-launch **Foresight coin** (2026-04-25 Follow-up #7) — a future mechanic for a Greek commander to reach an Aztec tower. Not launch scope.

The **OPEN BLOCKER discovery-mechanic question is CLOSED 2026-04-25**: Fusion is **menu-driven**, not positional. 9 locked recipes are visible in a codex from the Commander pick screen and from the in-match Fusion menu per [`concept/phase-4.md §4.3`](../concept/phase-4.md). Wiki-breaks-discovery is a non-issue because the recipes are surfaced in-game by design.

Hybrid-table target (4-8, 20-30, etc.) is **superseded** — launch skeleton is the **locked 9 Gods via 9 locked recipes**:
- Greek: Zeus (Hercules + Jason), Athena (Theseus + Perseus), Poseidon (Achilles + Odysseus)
- Aztec: Quetzalcoatl (Serpent-Priest + High Priest-King), Huitzilopochtli (Nanahuatzin + Jaguar Champion), Tezcatlipoca (Blood Priest + Eagle Champion)
- Norse: Odin (Harald Bluetooth + Leif Erikson), Thor (Björn Ironside + Beowulf), Tyr (Lagertha + Sigurd)

"Do hybrids evolve through tiers" is **moot**: a Fused God is the endgame; it does not tier-upgrade further (no T5/T6).

Earned 1 per boss round (mini-boss 5/15/25 + main-boss 10/20/30). Practical per-match reach: 2-3 Gods. MVP validation bar: 30%+ of playtesters reach at least one Fusion per match per [`concept/phase-6.md §6.4`](../concept/phase-6.md).

## Scope

Cross-lineage hybrid units that unlock via board adjacency. Covers:

- Adjacency rules (what counts as "adjacent," duration threshold).
- Hybrid tech tree — how hybrids appear in the build menu once unlocked.
- Hybrid table — 20–30 entries spanning all age × lineage-pair combinations.
- Adjacent-lineage (common) vs opposite-lineage (rare, high-payoff) hybrids.
- The discovery mechanic — **OPEN BLOCKER from [CONCEPT §4.3](../CONCEPT.md)**. Three candidates: accept-rote, randomness, per-commander/per-account gating.
